About me

At the age of only 16, my high school education with a focus on English, mathematics, physics and computer science provides a solid foundation for my passion for programming. Knowledge of C++ is not just a skill for me - it is the language in which I express my most innovative ideas. My experience in this field is the greatest, but my curiosity knows no limits, which led me to also explore Python, JavaScript and Frontend technology. Although I'm at the beginning of this path, I can already see huge potential in the Git environment, which fascinates me with its flexibility and possibilities. Traveling around the world is another chapter of my book of experiences that teaches me flexibility and openness to new cultures. I am a person who is constantly searching, exploring and not afraid of challenges.

My bio is just an introduction to a story that is still being written - a story full of codes, depths and countless paths that are still ahead of me. Are you ready to join my journey?
